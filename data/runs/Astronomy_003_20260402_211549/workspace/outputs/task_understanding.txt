Task objective:
Analyze synthetic numerical-relativity quality-control datasets for an SXS-style binary black hole catalog, with the goal of characterizing waveform accuracy and uncertainty patterns relevant to gravitational-wave modeling, catalog validation, and extrapolation/mode-selection decisions.

Available data and context:
- INSTRUCTIONS.md defines a full research workflow and final deliverables inside this workspace.
- data/fig6_data.csv: 1500 single-valued waveform-difference measurements representing highest-resolution mismatch after alignment.
- data/fig7_data.csv: 1500x7 modal waveform-difference measurements for spherical-harmonic modes ell=2...8.
- data/fig8_data.csv: 1200x2 extrapolation-order difference measurements comparing N=2 vs N=3 and N=2 vs N=4.
- related_work/ contains four papers giving scientific context on SXS waveforms, numerical-relativity accuracy/systematics, surrogate modeling, higher-mode/ringdown effects, and eccentric-binary applications.

Main planned analysis steps:
1. Load and validate all CSV files, confirm dimensions, column meanings, and summary statistics.
2. Perform exploratory distribution analysis of waveform differences using log-scaled descriptive plots and robust statistics (median, quantiles, tail behavior).
3. Compare error behavior across harmonic modes, including central tendency growth with ell, spread, and outlier structure.
4. Compare extrapolation-order discrepancy distributions between N2vsN3 and N2vsN4, including relative shifts and convergence interpretation.
5. Synthesize findings into implications for catalog accuracy, mode truncation, and waveform reliability, with clear limitations because the inputs are synthetic summary data rather than full simulations.
6. Produce reproducible code, saved intermediate outputs, publication-style figures, and a final report.

Expected deliverables:
- Reproducible analysis scripts in code/.
- Saved numerical summaries/intermediate outputs in outputs/.
- Figures in report/images/ covering data overview, main results, and validation/comparison plots.
- Final research report in report/report.md interpreting the numerical-relativity accuracy trends shown by these datasets.
