Task objective: Analyze monolayer epitaxial graphene tr-ARPES measurements under 5 μm mid-infrared pumping to provide direct energy- and momentum-resolved evidence for Floquet-Bloch replica bands of the Dirac cone, and assess how polarization-dependent signals support a mechanism involving photon-dressed Volkov final states.

Available data in this workspace: (1) raw_trARPES_data.h5 with energy axis, kx axis, time delays, pump-off spectrum, and pump-on spectra for seven pump polarization angles; (2) processed_band_data.json with the extracted Dirac point, replica-band positions/intensities, full band-dispersion picks, axes, and pump parameters; (3) polarization_dependence_data.csv with replica-band intensity versus polarization angle; (4) related_work/ contains four reference PDFs for scientific context.

Main planned analysis steps: inspect the raw HDF5 structure and compare pump-off versus pump-on spectra; visualize the main Dirac cone and ±1 replica bands in energy-momentum space; quantify replica-band energy offsets, momentum locations, and intensity relative to the main cone using the processed JSON; analyze the angular dependence from the CSV and test whether it is consistent with the expected polarization response; cross-check extracted features against the raw spectra and interpret the results in the Floquet-Bloch/Volkov-state framework described by the related work.

Expected deliverables: reproducible analysis code in code/, intermediate numerical summaries and figures in outputs/, and a publication-style report at report/report.md with referenced figures saved in report/images/, including data overview plots, main Floquet-replica result figures, polarization/validation plots, and discussion of methodology, evidence, and limitations.
